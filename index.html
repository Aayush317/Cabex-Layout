At CabexIndia, our vision is to be the leading provider of high-quality Industrial Cable Gland, Industrial Cable Connectors, Earthing Accessories & Lightning Protection Accessories, offering unmatched reliability and safety for our customers.

CabexIndia has established a noteworthy market presence in various countries, including Europe, UK, Australia, the Middle East, and the Far East. Our product range includes Cable Glands for both industrial and hazardous areas, Thread Conversion & Blanking Plugs, as well as Earthing & Lightning Protection Accessories. Moreover, we have the capability to create tailor-made electrical components & electric cable connectors according to our clients’ specifications.
